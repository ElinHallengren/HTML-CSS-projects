Introduction

Food is many things. It is what gives us energy to do the things that we want to do. It is the building blocks to help us grow, both from child to adult, and to increase muscle mass, fat tissue, and blood. Food can for many also be comfort, a friend when we are lonely, a way to show and recieve love.

Pro-tip

Food is neccessary to stay alive, but it can also be what is killing you.

In this document food means the building blocks that is absorbed through the bowelsystem, and is what your whole body consists of.

What is food?

Food consists of five major components. Three that are energy-giving and two that are just as important, but they do not provide energy.

  1. Carbohydrates
  2. Fat
  3. Protein
  4. Vitamins
  5. Minerals

The first three, the energy-giving catagories, are what we will continue discussing. The last two, vitamins and minerals, are important for life, and if you don't get enough of them, you will get very sick. In vitamins and minerals are also antioxidants included. In this document, we will continue talking about the three energy-givers, how to properly combine them for proper health and benefit.

Pro-tip

If you combine the energy-giving components correctly, there is no need to worry about vitamins and minerals, and there is no need to take suplements!

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are our main energy source.

Pro-tip

Become friendly with your carbohydrates, don't skip them!

Carbohydrates for life!

Fat

Fat is our primary stored energy.

Pro-tip

Some fat is neccessary, but too much will make you store more fat.

Lose fat tissue with low intensity exercise and lots of water!

Protein

Protein is the buildingblocks for our muscles, hormones and our immunesystem.

Pro-tip

Protein is where your focus should be if you want to stay full and still lose fat tissue, whilst maintaining lean tissue.

Celebrate protein and have some with every meal!
